Did you know?

  1. Elephants are very social:
    • Elephants live in family units called herds.
    • Herds are led by a matriarch, the oldest and most experienced female.
    • Elephant families often help each other raise young calves.

  2. Elephants are the world's largest land animal:
    • They can eat up to 300 pounds of food per day.
    • They are one of the only mammals that cannot jump.

  3. An elephant never forgets:
    • Elephants have an excellent memory.
    • They communicate through vibrations.
    • They are one of the few animals that can recognize themselves in a mirror.
